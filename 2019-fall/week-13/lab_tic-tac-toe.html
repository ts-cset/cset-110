CSET-110 Lab:
Tic-Tac-Toe

Today, you're going to going to build Tic-Tac-Toe.

Grab a partner, spin up a git repo, and start planning. Here are the rules. Don't add any new features if you don't have to!

Requirements

  • Two players, X and O, face off in the classic battle of wits.
  • The game opens with an empty grid of nine squares, three rows by three columns.
  • The first turn should be randomized, but after that, each player takes their turn placing their symbol in an open square.
  • The game ends when one player owns three consecutive squares, either by row, column, or diagonal. The game can also end in a stalemate if all the squares are filled without a winner.
  • The winner, or a stalemate, should be declared and the players should have the option of reseting the board and playing again.
